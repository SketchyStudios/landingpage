My Iceland Trip

I went to Iceland in November of 2023. The landscape felt like I was on the planet Hoth from Star Wars. Here's my pictures from there.

Some of my pictures from my trip!

A statue of Leif Erikson, discoverer of Vinland. Hallgrimmskirkja is the building behind it. It's also the tallest building in iceland.

A Tandori chicken naan tower with a sushi background. Food is from Posthus in Reykjavik.

More food from Posthus, this time buttered chicken bowl and a pizza!

I would have never guessed that Iceland is home of the worlds best hotdog. Do yourself a favor and try one when you're there.
